Extra Nerd Stuff

▾
  • My icon is a fusion of two Nordic runes: Algiz (protection) and Kaunaz (creativity and fire). My username borrows from Vargr, Old Norse for “wolf.” Combine that with my love of cold, dark northern countries and you get... this.

  • Fun fact: none of the text on this site is pure white. It’s actually a very light gray with a subtle outline, for readability and accessibility. I hope the little accessibility touches help someone - even if I’m mostly fumbling around without feedback.

  • Why the bar crawl theme? Because it made me laugh. I don’t even drink alcohol.
