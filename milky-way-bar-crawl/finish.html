Sanity Check

▸

Before proceeding further into the night, it’s worth doing a quick sanity check on whichever game you just modded.

  • Start a new game and get acquainted with the Mod Settings menu.
  • Play for at least 15–20 minutes in that game.
    For example:
    • LE1: reach Saren’s introduction on Eden Prime.
    • LE2: make it to Jacob on Lazarus Station.
    • LE3: get to the beacon in the harbour.
  • Spend a bit of time in a busy area (e.g. Citadel) and keep an eye on:
    • crashes or infinite loading screens,
    • unusual performance dips.
  • If you spot something odd, check the FAQ first, then the comments on the Nexus guide.
    All else fails, reach out to me.

If your test run feels good, consider this your last clean round. From here on out, any new mods are house rules - add them only if you're ready to troubleshoot.
