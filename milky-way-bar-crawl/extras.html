Milky Way Bar Crawl - Extras

Time To Choose

▾

Welcome to the Extras Section!

Here you will find mods that could prove to be a good addition to your list. Each one comes recommended, though I am not using all of them myself.
As such I can only offer limited support and need you to be extra vigilant in what you install and how to do so.

A Note on Appearance Mods

!

Many appearance mods require you to use the Trilogy Save Editor to give your Shepard these styles manually.
Be extra careful reading the instructions for these mods.

ME1

▾

A light sprinkling of flavor for a glass that is threatening to shatter if you look at it funny.

UNC Morality

Allows you to play both missions that are gated behind a high paragon/renegade score.

LE1 Definitive Audio Overhaul

Overhauls a ton of sounds by using those of later games.

Replenish Grenades LE1

Allows you to replenish your grenades from a locker on the Normandy.

Quick Loot (LE1)

Prevents the recovered items menu from appearing when looting.

Less Intense Vigil

Reduces the visual intensity of Vigil for those sensitive to flashing lights.

MorningAngel

&

ClericOfShadows

For all your custom Shepard and Armor needs let me direct you to the profiles of MorningAngel and ClericOfShadows who have done outstanding work there.

ME2

▾

Garnish for what many call their favorite. Which is good because the recipe list reads like a word search.

Dogtag Tracker

Adds a distance tracker to the Normandy Crash Site DLC.

Skip Kolyat Trailing Section

Skips the trailing section of Thane’s loyalty mission.
Note: This is more of a mod for repeat playthroughs.

Jacob Respects Thane and Tali

Removes lines from Jacob that could be construed as hostile towards Thane and Tali.

EGM Armors for LE2

Even more armors! Backported from EGM or Ark Mod.

No Headgear for Squadmates

Removes the headgear for your squadmates Alternate Appearance Pack armors.
Note: This mod has an optional texture file to reflect the changes in the squad selection screen. If you plan to texture mod your game later, grab this file.
Note: If you've been following along closely or went the batch route you need to reinstall Casual Hubs for the relevant patch.

Halcyon Hairpack (LE2)

New hairstyles based on designs from the Outer Worlds.
Note: This mod requires you to use the Trilogy Save Editor!

MorningAngel

&

ClericOfShadows

For all your custom Shepard and Armor needs let me direct you to the profiles of MorningAngel and ClericOfShadows who have done outstanding work there.
